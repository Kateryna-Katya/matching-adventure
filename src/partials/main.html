Game Description

Welcome to Jewels Magic - Mania Blast, a mesmerizing undersea puzzle game where you’ll explore the ocean depths and solve glittering match-3 challenges! Match sparkling gems, unlock chests filled with ancient riches, and dive into an unforgettable aquatic adventure.
Swap and blast your way through vibrant levels — from colorful coral reefs to haunting shipwrecks — as you complete quests, earn stars, and uncover hidden secrets. Whether you're a seasoned puzzle player or a casual gamer, this magical underwater world will keep you hooked!
